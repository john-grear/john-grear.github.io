This is a passion project of mine. It's a 2D magic platformer in Unity using C#. A lot of my inspiration comes from games like Hollow Knight, Mega Man, and a bit of Mario. I have been designing the game using Notion to lay out all my ideas and ensure this is a sound plan. I started working on development back in October 2023, but have been taking my time with it. I have been using free assets from Itch.io until I can start hiring people for asset and music development.

Once I have the game more developed and copyrights have been better firmly established, I can share more information. Icon was generated using AI since I am still without a cool icon.
