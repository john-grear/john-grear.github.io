# Wright State University

![Wright State University](/assets/img/wright-state.jpg)

I went to Wright State University (WSU) for my first two years of college, which significantly contributed to my academic and professional growth. As a Computer Science Teaching Assistant during my two years at Wright State, I had the opportunity to deepen my understanding of computer science concepts while assisting other students in their learning journeys. This role allowed me to solidify my comprehension of the subjects I was studying while developing strong communication and mentoring skills.

I transferred to BGSU after my second year (2021) when the COVID-19 outbreak was slowing down and classes began to be in person again.

# Bowling Green State University

![Bowling Green State University](/assets/img/bowling-green.jpg)

During my last two years of college at Bowling Green State University (BGSU), I delved into a diverse range of computer science courses, gaining a strong foundation in programming languages, algorithms, software development, and other fundamental aspects of the field. Engaging with my professors and fellow students broadened my understanding and honed my problem-solving skills, effectively preparing me for practical applications in the tech industry.
